Production Notes

Filming

  • Vertical format, 9:16
  • Natural gym or home gym setting
  • Talk to camera directly
  • Know the beats, improvise the words

Pacing

  • Scripts 1 and 4: primary long-form, film first
  • Scripts 2 and 3: short-form, film as B-roll coverage
  • Script 5: Q&A style, someone asks opener off-camera

Testing Order

  • Launch scripts 3 and 2 first (shortest, cheapest to test)
  • Promote the best-performing hook into the longer version
  • Script 5 runs as warm audience follow-up once data exists
